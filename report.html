4. Insight: Sex có ảnh hưởng đến Smoker?¶


${H_0}$: sex v smoker c lp nhau

${H_A}$: sex v smoker ph thuc nhau


t:

${A =}$ sex, ${A_1 =}$ male, ${A_2}$ = female

${B =}$ smoker, ${B_1 =}$ yes, ${B_2 =}$ no


Ta c:

${H_0}$: ${P(A_i\cap B_j) = P(A_i)P(B_j)}$

${H_A}$: ${P(A_i\cap B_j) \neq P(A_i)P(B_j)}$


Phn di s trnh by v mt ton hc ln s dng th vin scipy.stats tnh ton


In [32]:

Ta tnh c ${Pr(A_i)}$ nh bng trn v

${Pr(B_1)}$ = 0.2053838484546361

${Pr(B_2)}$ = 0.7946161515453639

n y ta c th tnh:

Gi tr mong i ${E}$:

\begin{equation} \text{Do k vng A v B c lp:}\\ E_{ij} = Pr(A_i) \times Pr(B_j) \times N [2]\\ \text{hay}\\ E_{ij} = \frac{\text{(Tng dng} \times \text{Tng ct)}}{\text{Tng bng}} [3] \\ \text{vi bng l bng contingency} \end{equation}

Gi tr ${\chi^2}$:

\begin{equation} \chi^2=\Sigma\frac{(O-E)^2}{E} [2][3]\\ \text{vi O l gi tr thc s v E l gi tr mong i} \end{equation}

Gi tr dof: Degree of freedom

dof cho ${\chi^2}$ c lp:

\begin{equation} dof = v = rc - 1 - (r-1) - (c-1) = (r-1)(c-1) [2]\\ = 1 \end{equation}

Chn mc ngha:

\begin{equation} \alpha = 0.05 \end{equation}

Tra bng Chi Squared vi ${\alpha = 0.05, dof = 1}$ ta c critical value ${ = 3.841459}$

Chp nhn ${H_0}$ nu \begin{equation} \chi^2_v <= 3.841459 \end{equation}


Ta c th s dng chi2_contingency ca th vin spicy tnh ton, cc gi tr tnh c t th vin v kt lun l:

p-value l: 0.09827321674727184

chi = 2.733346, critical value = 3.841459

Vi mc ngha 0.05, ta bc b HA v chp nhn H0.

Kt lun: sex v smoker c lp.


Ta kim tra, khng dng th vin, c kt qu nh sau:

II. Thuật toán sử dụng¶

Trong lab ny, nhm em s dng 2 thut ton chnh l Simple linear regression v Support Vector Regression (SVR) (l mt dng m rng ca Support Vector MachineMachine).

1. Cách thức đánh giá mô hình¶

  • Nhm em s dng do R-squared nh gi m hnh
  • Phng php ly mu nh gi m hnh l K-Fold Cross-Validation vi k = 10

2. Thuật toán SVR¶

a. Giới thiệu SVR¶

Support Vector Machine (SVM)

SVM l bi ton phn lp, v i tm mt phn cch sao cho margin tm c l ln nht, ng ngha vi vic cc im d liu an ton nht so vi mt phn cch.

Support Vector Regression (SVR)

SVR l mt bin th ca SVM dng cho bi ton hi quy. SVR tm cch cc tiu margin sao cho c th cha nhiu im d liu nht c th.
